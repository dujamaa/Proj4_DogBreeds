Dog Breed Classifier

Capstone Project for Udacity's Data Science Nanodegree

This web-app takes a user-supplied image as input. If a dog is detected in the image, it will provide an estimate of the dog's breed. If a human is detected in the image, it will provide an estimate of the dog breed that is most resembling. If neither a dog nor a human is detected in the image, it will provide output that indicates an error.
Click on the "Browse" button to select an image to classify, then click the "Classify Image" button:
